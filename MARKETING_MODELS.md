**Purpose:** Compare **four** launch paths — **Meta‑first vs TikTok Shop‑first**, each **with or without** an agency — so you can pick a **coherent strategy** for **social content** (**human retainers + AI UGC tools**) and **paid ads** on a **$30k** marketing budget. The numbers tie to **published benchmarks** (with citations); they are **planning aids**, not forecasts.

**Inventory (updated):** You can **replenish product on demand** at **no incremental cash cost** to this plan, **up to 5,000 units**. **426** remains useful as **starting on‑hand** stock; it is **no longer** a hard ceiling on samples + sales for launch modeling.

- **Not modeled:** chargebacks, returns, sales tax, payment processor on D2C (assume in margin or add ~3%), packaging inserts, and **LTV**. **Interest:** **$0** during **0% intro**; after month 12, refinance or pay down.

**Locked planning line for Models 5 & 6:** **$5,000 per 60 days** (mid–high band) — covers credit overruns and a serious **avatar / lip‑sync** tool, which is what made the **LAIFE** result repeatable.

**Per‑clip implied cost @ planning line:** $5,000 ÷ **2,000 clips** (1,000/mo × 2) = **~$2.50/clip** — at the **lean** end of the $2–$40 industry band, possible only because **labor is in‑house ($0 cash)**.

### What 1,000/mo unlocks

- **Testing velocity:** 200+ active variants/week → matches the LAIFE‑style Shop algorithm needs (**50+ weekly variations**) and lifts ROAS via faster winner discovery.
- **Hook iteration:** Same product → many angles (energy, focus, sleep, pre‑workout, etc.) without re‑shooting.
- **Caveat (from research anchors):** AI clips **fatigue ~50% faster** than human UGC and CTR runs **~0.13 pp lower** — the **volume itself** is the moat. If you slow the pipeline, paid efficiency reverts.

### Compliance & disclosure flags (must‑read before launch)

- **Meta:** Requires advertisers to disclose **digitally created or altered** content for **social issues, elections, politics**; non‑political AI content has **no universal disclosure rule** today, but supplement **claims** still fall under Meta's prohibited‑content rules. Avoid AI avatars **impersonating doctors / experts**.
- **TikTok:** **AI‑Generated Content (AIGC) labeling** is **required** for realistic AI content (auto‑labeled by some tools; manually toggled otherwise). TikTok Shop adds **supplement‑specific** restricted‑claim rules.
- **FTC:** Endorsements made via AI avatars must still reflect **honest opinions and real experience** — using a synthetic "user testimonial" without a real underlying user is a **deceptive endorsement** risk.

## Practical conclusions

1. **Scarce input = $30k marketing**, not inventory. Models 1–4 fix ~$5.6k for human retainers; Models 5–6 swap that for **$5k of AI tooling at scale** to push ad budget to **$24k / $22k** respectively.
2. **AI UGC at 1,000 clips/mo (Models 5–6)** is the **only path to a plausibly first‑order‑positive scenario** in the doc — **Model 6 at $17 CPA ≈ +$1,456** vs **all other models still negative** at planning CPAs. The result hinges on **TikTok health‑category CPA benchmarks** holding up and **AI fatigue** being managed by sheer **iteration velocity**.
3. **Meta / D2C (Models 1, 2, 5)** keeps **~$25.80** contribution per unit before marketing; **TTS (Models 3, 4, 6)** is **~$19.66** (paid) / **~$15.04** (affiliate) / **~$17.35** blended after fully‑loaded TTS fees.
4. **Human vs AI tradeoff (research):** AI delivers **~−13% CPA** in health & wellness on Meta and matches/beats human creative on TikTok at scale, **but** loses **0.13 pp CTR** and **fatigues ~50% faster** — pure‑AI models (5, 6) need **weekly winning‑creative refresh** to hold the math.
5. **Compliance on Models 5–6 is non‑optional:** TikTok requires **AIGC labels**, supplement claims still fall under FDA/FTC scrutiny regardless of avatar source, and FTC treats fake AI testimonials as **deceptive endorsements**. Build a **claims‑review SOP** before scaling AI volume.
6. **Two founders' cards @ 0% for 12 months** — finance cost ≈ $0 if paid within promo; calendar payoff/refinance before revert APR.
7. **TTS scenarios** still assume **250 seeds/mo** ($2k ship / 60d) — including Model 6, because affiliate program lift comes from real creators using real product.
8. **Recommended sequencing (risk‑adjusted):** Run **Model 4** (humans + small AI) for **30 days** to validate Shop ops, affiliate response, and creative learnings. If creative throughput becomes the bottleneck and TTS CPA trends below **~$30**, **transition to Model 6** for the next 30 days with proven data — keeping ~$5–10k of card capital as a reload buffer.
